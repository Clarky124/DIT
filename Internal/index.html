INFO

I have a DJI Spark that I use to take nice cinematic air shots and aerial photos. I have a Canon Powershot SX60 HS for filming long high quality videos, vlogging and other things. I also use my iPhone 8 Plus to take lots of photos on the go. I have a DJI Osmo Mobile 2 that helps me take nice stable videos with my phone. It is good for hyper lapses and cinematic shots on the ground. I have worked on various projects, including as a wedding videographer, and the official videographer for the 2019 Arrowtown Autumn Festival.

This is a map of where I can fly my drone in the Queenstown Area:


Red = No fly zone

Blue = No fly unless permission from the owner of the airspace

Grey first zone = No higher than 60m

Grey second zone = No higher than 100m

Anywhere else = No restrictions apart from flying over private property
